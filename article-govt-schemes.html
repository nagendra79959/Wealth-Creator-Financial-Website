PPF, NPS, Sukanya — Complete Telugu Guide

Government-backed investment schemes అన్నీ ఒక చోట. PPF, NPS, Sukanya Samriddhi, PM Yojanas — benefits, eligibility, మరియు how to apply — simple Telugu లో.

🏛️ Key Takeaways

  • Government schemes లో investment 100% safe — government guarantee ఉంటుంది
  • PPF — best long-term safe investment, 15 years, tax-free maturity
  • NPS — retirement savings కి best, extra ₹50,000 tax deduction
  • Sukanya Samriddhi — girl child కి, current rate 8.2%, tax free
  • EPF — salaried employees కి automatic, employer matching benefit

🏛️ Government Schemes ఎందుకు Important?

Government-backed schemes are 100% safe because they're backed by the Government of India. Unlike stocks or mutual funds, there's no market risk. The returns are fixed and guaranteed — perfect for the risk-averse investor or as a stable base for your portfolio.

Government schemes అంటే — government తన గ్యారంటీ ఇస్తుంది. Bank collapse అయినా, market crash అయినా — మీ money safe. Returns fixed గా announce అవుతాయి. వీటిని portfolio లో base గా use చేయండి.

📋 Top Government Schemes — Details

🏦
PPF (Public Provident Fund)
Current Rate: 7.1% p.a. (Tax-free)
₹500 నుండి ₹1.5L per year invest చేయవచ్చు. 15 year lock-in. Maturity amount + interest completely tax-free. Section 80C benefit.
Tax-Free Returns
👴
NPS (National Pension System)
Expected: 8–10% p.a.
Retirement కి invest చేయడానికి best. Extra ₹50,000 tax deduction (80CCD(1B)). Equity + Bonds mix. Age 60 వరకు lock-in.
Retirement Planning
👧
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
Current Rate: 8.2% p.a. (Highest!)
Girl child కి (under 10 years). ₹250 నుండి start చేయవచ్చు. 21 years maturity. Tax-free. Section 80C benefit. Marriage / education కి use చేయవచ్చు.
Girl Child Scheme
💼
EPF (Employee Provident Fund)
Current Rate: 8.15% p.a.
Salaried employees కి automatic. Salary లో 12% EPF కి వెళ్తుంది, employer మరో 12% match చేస్తారు. Retirement కి powerful savings tool.
Salaried Employees
📮
NSC (National Savings Certificate)
Current Rate: 7.7% p.a.
Post office లో available. 5 year lock-in. Guaranteed returns. Section 80C benefit. Bank FD కంటే better rate.
Post Office Scheme
👵
Senior Citizen Savings Scheme
Current Rate: 8.2% p.a.
60+ age కి. ₹15L వరకు invest. Quarterly interest payment. Bank FD కంటే higher rate. Section 80C benefit.
Seniors Only
✅ Best Strategy: Combine schemes based on your goal

Retirement కి: EPF + NPS + PPF combination. Girl child కి: Sukanya. Safe guaranteed returns కి: NSC లేదా Post Office FD. Tax saving కి: PPF + ELSS combination best.

❌ Don't put ALL money in government schemes

Government schemes safe కానీ returns తక్కువ (7–8%). Inflation (6%) తర్వాత real return చాలా తక్కువ అవుతుంది. Portfolio లో 30–40% government schemes, 60–70% equity (mutual funds/stocks) balance maintain చేయండి.
