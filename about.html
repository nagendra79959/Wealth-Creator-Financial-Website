👤 About Creator

Meet Wealth Creator

Welcome! I'm the face behind Wealth Creator — a Telugu finance educator with a passion for making complex financial concepts simple and accessible for every Indian investor.

Born and raised in Andhra Pradesh, I've experienced firsthand how lack of financial education holds people back from building wealth. That's why I started this channel — to bridge that gap for Telugu-speaking investors across India and the world.

🎯 My Mission

Why I Started Wealth Creator

Growing up in a middle-class Telugu family, I saw how hard-working people struggled financially simply because they didn't know about investing, compound interest, or tax saving. Banks offered negligible returns, and the stock market felt like a foreign concept.

When I discovered the power of SIP investing and the stock market, I realized I had to share this knowledge in our language — Telugu. That's when Wealth Creator was born.

My goal: Help 1 million Telugu families achieve financial freedom through education, not expensive courses or false promises.
