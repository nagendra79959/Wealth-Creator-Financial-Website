Privacy Policy

How Wealth Creator collects, uses, and protects your information

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Summary: Wealth Creator is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data, never sell it to third parties, and you can request deletion at any time by contacting us at nagendra79959@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you interact with our website, you may provide:

  • Contact Form: Name, email address, phone number (optional), message content
  • Newsletter Subscription: Email address
  • Comments: Name and email if you comment on articles

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and messages
  • To send you our newsletter (only if you subscribed)
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and popular content
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

3. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (dark/light mode preference, session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (anonymized)
  • YouTube Cookies: When you view embedded YouTube videos, YouTube may set cookies per their privacy policy

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • YouTube (Google): Embedded video content
  • Google Fonts: Typography (font loading)
  • Telegram: Channel communications

Each third party has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Newsletter subscribers' emails are retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are retained for 1 year.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Unsubscribe: Opt-out of marketing emails at any time
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at nagendra79959@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance.
