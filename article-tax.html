📌 Tax Save అంటే ఏమిటి?

Tax saving means legally reducing the amount of income tax you pay to the government. The Indian Income Tax Act gives you several deductions — if you invest in certain instruments, that investment amount is subtracted from your taxable income.

Tax saving అంటే government కి తక్కువ tax చెల్లించడం — ఇది illegal కాదు, completely legal. మీరు ELSS, PPF, LIC లో invest చేస్తే, ఆ amount మీ taxable income నుండి తగ్గిపోతుంది. అంటే మీ tax తగ్గుతుంది. ఇది మీ హక్కు!
