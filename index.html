Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wealth Creator

What topics does Wealth Creator cover? +
Wealth Creator covers a wide range of finance topics in Telugu: Stock Market analysis and trading tips, SIP & Mutual Fund investment strategies, Dividend Stock picks for passive income, Government Schemes like PPF, NPS, Sukanya Samriddhi, Personal Finance and budgeting, Insurance planning, and Tax Saving strategies under Section 80C.
How often are new videos uploaded? +
We upload new videos consistently — typically 3-4 videos per week. Subscribe to the YouTube channel and tap the bell 🔔 icon to get instant notifications for every new video. We also share daily market updates and investment tips.
Is this channel suitable for beginners in stock market? +
Absolutely! Wealth Creator is designed specifically for Telugu-speaking investors at all levels. We have dedicated beginner playlists covering the basics of stock market, how to open a Demat account, what is SIP, and step-by-step investment guides. You don't need any prior knowledge to start learning.
How can I start SIP investing with a small amount? +
You can start SIP with as little as ₹100-₹500 per month! We have detailed Telugu videos explaining how to select the right mutual fund, calculate SIP returns using our guides, and start through apps like Groww, Zerodha Coin, or Paytm Money. Watch our "SIP Beginners Guide" playlist for step-by-step instructions.
Do you provide personalized investment advice? +
Wealth Creator provides general finance education and information. We do not provide SEBI-registered personalized investment advice. All videos are for educational purposes only. Please consult a qualified financial advisor for personalized investment decisions based on your individual financial situation.
How can I contact Wealth Creator? +
You can reach us through our Contact Page, join our Telegram channel for daily updates, or DM us on Instagram. For business collaborations or sponsorship inquiries, please use the contact form and we'll respond within 2-3 business days.
